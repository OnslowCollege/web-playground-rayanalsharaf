Liverpool History

The start

Liverpool's Football club was founded in 1892 and is now known as of one England's greatest football teams.

The legend Bill Shankly

The rise of Liverpool started in the 1960s when manager Bill Shankly rebuilt the club and made the frame of a staircase for what achievments the club would accomplish.
